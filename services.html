Our Services and Conditions

Minimum Age

The minimum age for renting cars 25 years. Drivers under the age of 25 years can rent a car by purchasing additional insurance.

Fuel

The car is issued to the lessee with a full or partially full tank of fuel. Return the car should be with the same amount of fuel in the tank, with which the car was received.

24/7 Support

All our clients have the opportunity to receive emergency assistance 24 hours a day. The phone is listed in the rental contract.

What do you need to have with yourself

    All drivers must have a driving license with a validity period of at least 3 years.
    Passport or identity card (DNI) is required on a mandatory basis

Aditional Costs

    Babies seat cost 4 eur daily
    Booster cost 2 eur daily
    An additional driver is issued on site (5 € per day, a separate booking is not required).

Booking and Payment

The application for booking is confirmed within 24 hours (on working days) by e-mail.

Important additional information

    WHAT IS NOT INCLUDED IN THE RENTAL PRICE?
  • All tenants are responsible for penalties for speeding, parking tickets and other traffic violations, as well as all administrative costs associated with these penalties.
  • Loss or damage to the ignition key. Pay the full replacement of the expense of the tenant.
  • Penalty for cleaning heavily soiled car. Regardless of your Lease Agreement, you will be required to pay the cost of the work that exceeds the standard cleaning or cleaning of the cabin (child seats), in order to restore their condition at the time of the start of the lease.
      • CALCULATION OF COST
  • Price is calculated by the date and time of receipt and return of the vehicle and in line with the tenant before the lease.
  • If you decide to get a car or return it later before the agreed period, recalculated for the unused time is not made and the money is not returned.
  • At high season, from 01 august till 31 august & from 23 december till 09 january, price per day can be changed. In this case, we inform the client about the exact price, before confirming the reservation.
      • IMPORTANT
  • It is not allowed to use the car for commercial purposes; transporting animals; transfer control car to persons not entered in the contract; control the machine in alcohol, narcotic intoxication and under the influence of medicines; overload the car; tow another vehicle or go in tow by yourself.
